MY NEW YORK ESSAY LOCATION DIRECTIONS

ADDRESS: 136-20 ROOSEVELT AVE 3F, FLUSHING, NY 11354

1. Take the E, F, M, or R lines to Jackson Heights-Roosevelt Ave.

2. Transfer to the 7 train headed to Flushing-Main Street (17 minutes). Get off at the last stop.

3. Walk 151 ft (1 minute) to Grand Mall.

4. Take the escalators to the top floor to reach Royal Queen Restaurant.
